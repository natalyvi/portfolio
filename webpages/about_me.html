I was born in a small city in Colombia, since I was a little kid I was interested in how the things we use in everyday life are made and when I grew up, I decided to study Mechanical Engineering because of the design orientation it had.

When I finished all my classes I went to do an internship at an Ice Factory and I discovered my passion for the optimization of existing processes.

After 4 years I moved to the US and worked on the service industry and as a general manager for a couple of years, I learned a lot of communication skills and how to work in teams and achieve goals.

I moved on with my career and worked on quality control in a car manufacturer company, that job teached me to spot hard to find defects and to work under deadlines.

Every one of those experiences took me to the point I am right now, where I am able to use on a daily basis every lesson I learned, enjoying what I do.
